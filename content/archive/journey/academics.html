Academics

Learn it the right way.

Learn with English to learn English. Coming to Kinglee, the biggest change is the learning objective. We don’t only learn English here; we learn knowledge using English. Be prepared to use English in class daily, no matter if it’s reading, writing, speaking, or listening.

Start early. Stop procrastinating! Start your work today, now! Starting assignments early will provide you so many advantages. It not only gives you time for revising your work, it also provides time for communication with teachers as well as flexibility for your schedule. Summer and winter homework, especially, should be completed in advance, instead of the day before the school starts.

Stay on top of your homework. Doing homework may sound simple; nevertheless, it is one of the most important ways to improve your grade. By completing homework on time with quality, you demonstrate your effort outside of the classroom. Because there are so much homework in a semester, take the opportunity and get your grade boosted!

Your grade matters. Many are worried about standardized test scores when applying to colleges, but it is not the only factor that admission officers consider. Your high school grades, especially, are considered one of the most important factors. Having a good academic record is the best way to demonstrate your academic capabilities. Consequently, working on your school work regularly will help you a lot when applying to colleges.
