Problem

People, unprotected.

Seattle has a low flu vaccination rate. Despite the availability of flu shot, many people are unvaccinated, lacking flu protection. In Washington along, only 51.1% of adult are vaccinated, having half of the population unprotected.


Causes of low vaccination rate. - Fear of negative impact on health
- Inconvenient physical location of vaccination
- High cost of vaccination
- Lack of awareness of the need of vaccination
- Lack of vaccination norm
