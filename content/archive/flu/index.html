Welcome to
the flu season.

Flu, or influenza, is one of the most influential diseases in the human history. As the flu season has come for Seattle, adequate disease prevention measures are needed to minimize the adverse effects caused by flu.

Prevention.

Supported by numerous studies, flu vaccine is the most effective and safe prevention method that can reduce flu symptoms, if not preventing flu infection. Despite its effectiveness, Seattle still has a low flu vaccination rate that can pose risks to people who are vulnerable to major diseases.

2020 Flu Shot Campaign.

Hosted by medical centers in Seattle, the campaign aims to raise public awareness of flu shot and to provide a more convenient flu shot experience. With the support of vaccine trucks, flu shot will only be a few blocks away from your home.
