The Great Gatsby Eulogy

Swallow (Class of 2020)

4 Jun. 2018

Eulogy

Alas the man with dreams so great,

Whose ambition was deeper than the sea.

The man whose life glittered with hope,

Like stars shining through a dark night sky.

Alas the man frozen in time,

The past replaying forever in his mind.

The man whose dreams were covered in a veil,

And filled with fantasy and wishes.

Alas the Great American Dreamer,

Whose life will be a legend of memories.
