Dreams

Swallow

20 Apr. 2018

Poetry

Dreams are the magic that let you escape the real world,

They bring you to a place where there isn't any worries or fear.

So lay your head down on your pillow and close your eyes,

And let your dreams take you to a faraway land way up high.

Across the rainbow bridge and up into the glorious heavens,

Oh hark! Hear the angels sing on the golden streets of Jerusalem.

But we can't stop for long, we must keep going, to the galaxy!

The universe is huge, you can't imagine how many stars exist out here,

Dazzling and sparkling all night for millions of years watching over you.

Ah, look at our Earth, so tiny isn't it, a globe of blue, green and white.

Red Mars, beautiful Venus, silver Mercury and Saturn with her many rings,

Jupiter the gas giant, blue Neptune, pale Uranus and poor little Pluto.

You see, out of all these amazing planets only our Earth has life,

Yet we do not treasure it, this marvelous gift, we waste it, ruin it.

So will you just dream these dreams or do something to make it real?
