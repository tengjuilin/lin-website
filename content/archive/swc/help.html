Find ways to improve your browsing and publishing experiences.

Window Size Compatibility

For your best browing experience, please use 9.7 inch iPad or above in landscape mode.

Bilibili Video Player

For your best streaming experience, please use Google Chrome if your are using iOS devices.

Bilibili player could not steam videos in Safari and Firefox on iOS devices.

Bilibili player could not be full screened on iOS devices.

Bilibili player could only steam videos with low resolution.

Html 5 Video Player

Html 5 video player could not steam videos under school Wi-Fi.

Html 5 video player could steam videos with high resolution.

窗口大小支持

为保证您的浏览体验，请使用9.7英寸iPad或以上横屏浏览.

Bilibili视频播放器

为保证您的视频播放体验，请在iOS设备上使用Google Chrome浏览器.

Bilibili视频播放器暂时无法在iOS设备上的Safari或Firefox上播放.

Bilibili视频播放器暂时无法在iOS设备上全屏.

Bilibili视频播放器仅能播放低分辨率视频.

Html 5 视频播放器

Html 5 视频播放器无法在学校Wi-Fi网络下播放视频.

Html 5 视频播放器可以播放高分辨率视频.
