Student Original Work Publication Center

Publish your amazing work and view others'

SWC is a platform for work publication that allows students to publish their fabulous work and enables students to learn from each other. All types of work that fall in the categories of art and writing can be published on SWC.

Publish

Student Original Work Publication Center is designed for you to publish your very own work. Your work could be in the categories of art and writing. View the submission guideline to see the information that you should provide.
