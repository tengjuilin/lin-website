About Polarize

Polarize is a website that was designed by Owen Lin during his Winter break. The main goal of this website is to help students to organize their learning materials and provide a platform for everyday learning of vocabularies.

The website is designed in two languages, English and Chinese. The English part is the main part of the website, which provides the information for everyday learning and student council information. The Chinese part is for those who are now in junior high level, which needs some Chinese support for student council instructions. The Chinese part only provides student council information in Kinglee High School.
